We wanted to let you know that because of all of your kind generosity, we decided to donate a portion of our wedding gifts to a non-profit that is near and dear to our hearts, DonorsChoose.org (Andy was also lucky enough to work for them in NYC). As you all know, the U.S. government keeps cutting down funding for public education. This is something we are concerned about for our future generation and we really admire what DonorsChoose.org is doing to try to alleviate this issue. Their crowd-funding model helps fund low-income, high poverty public schools and allows benefactors to donate any amount they would like to different projects that teachers would like to implement in their curriculum. After we donated a portion of our wedding fund, we really enjoyed how the teachers and students sent us pictures and letters showing us how they spent the money we gave them. We've also included a gallery to show you what your generosity has done for these children. Please don't hesitate to donate more by clicking this link.
